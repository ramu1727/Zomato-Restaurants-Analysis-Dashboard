## 🍽️ Zomato Restaurants Analysis Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview

Analyzed Zomato restaurant data to uncover insights about food trends and customer preferences

Built an interactive dashboard using Power BI for better visualization and decision-making

Focused on restaurant distribution, cuisines, ratings, and service availability

## 🎯 Objectives

Identify popular cuisines and restaurant distribution across cities

Analyze customer preferences using ratings and votes

Evaluate adoption of online delivery and table booking services

Provide insights to improve business strategies in the food industry

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation

Excel / CSV – Data preprocessing and cleaning

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

Restaurant distribution across 15 countries and 141 cities

Cuisine-wise analysis to identify most popular food types

City-wise analysis highlighting restaurant concentration

Analysis of ratings and votes to understand customer preferences

Service analysis (online delivery and table booking availability)

## 🔍 Key Insights

North Indian cuisine has the highest number of restaurants

New Delhi has the highest restaurant concentration

Only 26% of restaurants offer online delivery

Only 12% provide table booking services

Significant variation in ratings across different restaurant chains

## 📈 Business Impact

Helps businesses identify high-demand cuisines and locations

Highlights gaps in service offerings like delivery and booking

Supports better decision-making for expansion and marketing strategies

Improves understanding of customer behavior and preferences
